OBSERVATIONS

The site contains no page labeled "Mission" or "Vision." In place of these labels, the site presents a values/beliefs framework and a branded phrase, "Information for Good®."
The term "CreditVision®" appears as a prefix across multiple distinct scoring products (Enhanced CreditVision® Score, CreditVision® New to Credit Score, CreditVision® CIBIL Commercial Rank, CreditVision® Algorithms).
Two separate domains exist with different content: cibil.com presents individual score/report checking, dispute resolution, and consumer FAQs; transunioncibil.com presents solutions and products framed for banks, NBFCs, and other institutional customers.
"Financial Inclusion" is listed as one of seven named solutions on the site, with three products (Grameen Score, CreditVision® New to Credit Score, CIBIL Microfinance Score) mapped specifically to it.
The homepage claims customers include "top banks, NBFCs and insurance firms," but no specific bank, NBFC, government, or industry-body partner names appear on the pages reviewed. The "Become a Member" page lists only categories of eligible members (Banks, NBFCs, FIs, HFCs, Insurance Companies, Telecom Companies, Credit Rating Agencies, SEBI, IRDA, Stock Brokers, Trading Members), not named entities.
TruIQ Analytics Studio is described as cloud-based and allows client data science teams to access it remotely to collaborate with TransUnion CIBIL's data science experts.
Three named products — Early Risk Score, Enhanced CreditVision® Score, and CreditVision® New to Credit Score — are each described in relation to the loan origination or borrower-acquisition stage rather than post-disbursement monitoring.
ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type 2, and PCI-DSS 4.0 certifications are displayed in the website footer, visible on every page reviewed.
