# Master Research Questions

## 1. Company
- Why does this company exist?
- What problem does it solve?
- How does it make money?
- Who are its customers?

## 2. Products
- What products exist?
- Which industries use them?
- What customer pain points do they solve?

## 3. Technology
- What technologies are publicly mentioned?
- Which engineering practices appear repeatedly?
- Which AI initiatives exist?

## 4. Hiring
- What roles are growing?
- What skills are repeatedly requested?
- What locations are hiring?

## 5. Leadership
- What themes appear in interviews?
- What future vision is discussed?

## 6. Industry
- Who are competitors?
- What trends are shaping this market?
- What regulations influence the business?

## 7. Opportunities
- Where could analytics create value?
- Where could AI create value?
- What problems remain unsolved?

## 8. Personal Insights
- What surprised me?
- What patterns did I notice?
- If I joined tomorrow, where could I contribute?
